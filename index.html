What Synkly does

Three jobs, handled automatically.

01 / Products

List once, everywhere

Push your catalog to TikTok Shop straight from Shopify or your store — titles, images, variants and pricing included.

02 / Inventory

Stock that never drifts

Sell a unit anywhere and every connected store reflects it within seconds — no more overselling out-of-stock items.

03 / Orders

How it works

Four steps to a mirrored store.

Step 1

Connect your store

Link your Shopify, Amazon, or Wix account in a couple of clicks. No code required.

Step 2

Connect TikTok Shop

Authorize Synkly through TikTok's official seller login — your data stays yours.

Step 3

Choose what syncs

Pick which products, prices and stock pools should mirror across both storefronts.

Step 4

Sit back

Synkly watches both stores continuously and keeps them in lockstep, automatically.
